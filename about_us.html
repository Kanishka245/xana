About Us

Dr. Kamal is a physician and scientist exploring the intersection of medicine, healthcare, and artificial intelligence. As an academic, speaker, author and thought leader, he provides insights into emerging medical technologies. His high-impact research and presentations at leading conferences demonstrate his expertise. With extensive medical practice and clinical trial experience, Dr. Kamal has a deep background in artificial intelligence, human-computer interaction, data analytics, product design, and consumer research. He and his cross-functional teams are advancing vaccine development, drug repositioning, cancer genomics, and COVID-19 treatments. Recently, Dr. Kamal's team pioneered an AI product for accelerated vaccine discovery and design. Previously, he developed the first data-driven clinical practice system, helping thousands of patients through his novel AI-powered care model. This paved the way for large-scale product development across clinical data and AI. Passionate, ambitious and quick-witted, Dr. Kamal earned a PhD in Complex Systems Science, an Advanced Postgraduate degree, and a Bachelor's in Medicine. With over 15 years of experience driving cutting edge products, bioinformatics pipelines, and major projects in vaccines, drug development, and oncology, he has consulted extensively on machine learning, digital workforce development, bioinformatics, robotic process automation, and healthcare services. Having worked with talented, international teams across the US, Europe, and the Middle East, Dr. Kamal has successfully supported over 10 clients by integrating change management and a transformational approach to reduce costs, increase revenue, and manage risks. He also mentors startups and provides strategic consultations to companies commercializing his lab's research services and SaaS products. .
