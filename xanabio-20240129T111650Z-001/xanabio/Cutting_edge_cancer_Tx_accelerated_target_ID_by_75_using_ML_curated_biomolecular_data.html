CASE STUDY

Cutting-edge cancer Tx accelerated target ID by 75% using ML & curated biomolecular data.

Key Highlights

  • DNA, evolving over time, accumulates distinct mutations, resulting in organized patterns with essential biological functions.
  • These patterns, spanning various genomic elements, provide valuable insights into DNA's regulatory and functional aspects.
  • Crucial to pattern formation are the physicochemical, mechanical, thermodynamic, and structural properties of DNA sequences, shaping their 3D structure.
  • Introducing DNASCANNER v2, an advanced algorithm in Python using the FLASK framework, it facilitates comprehensive analysis of 158 DNA properties. With a user-friendly interface, downloadable results, and interactive plots, it caters to non-specialized researchers. The tool covers aspects like mono/di/trinucleotide frequencies, structural features, physicochemical properties, thermodynamics, and mechanics of DNA sequences.
  • Demonstrating the utility of DNASCANNER v2, we analyze splice-site junctions, casposon insertion sequences, and transposon insertion sites in bacterial and human genomes. Additionally, a deep-learning module predicts potential transposon insertion sites in nucleotide sequences. Future plans include optimizing the prediction model through extensive training on larger datasets.
